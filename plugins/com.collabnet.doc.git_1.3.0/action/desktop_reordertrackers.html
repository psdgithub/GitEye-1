Reorder trackers

When you have multiple trackers in your project, you might want to reorder them to reflect the sequence in which you view the artifacts.

  1. In the CollabNet Sites view, right-click your project's Trackers, and select Reorder Trackers.
  2. In the Reorder Trackers dialog, use one of these options:
    • Sort them alphabetically.
    • Select a specific tracker and click Move Up or Move Down until the tracker is positioned where you want it.
  3. Click OK.
You can also reorder trackers in the CollabNet Sites tree using drag and drop.
