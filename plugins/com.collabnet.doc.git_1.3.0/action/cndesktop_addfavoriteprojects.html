Work with your favorite projects

By specifying your favorite projects, you can have only those projects displayed in the CollabNet Sites tree.

  • Specify your favorite projects.
    1. In the CollabNet Sites view, right-click a site and select Favorite Projects.
    2. Select your favorite projects from the list. By default, only the projects in which you're a member are displayed. To see a list of all projects on the site, de-select the Show my projects only option.
    3. Click OK.
  • To see only your favorite projects in the CollabNet Sites view, right-click the site and select Show > Favorite projects. Your other options are to see all projects in the site, or the ones in which you are a member.
